How does it work?
Use Case

Need to meet your lawyer, Joe, for lunch?

Ask your AI avatar to set up the meeting

Your Avatar and Joe's Avatar do the scheduling and let you guys know when and where to meet.

Forgot to setup the meeting with the factory floor?

Ask your AI Avatar to bail you out. He can send the last minute memo and send the calendar invites to everyone needed

See the use case...
Vision

Our plan is to scale our prototype so that every single person can build its own believable avatar, like in the movies.

The idea is that these avatars can interact with the internet on their own and do the mundane communications and operations on your behalf.

Our Vision

  • We first solve several smaller problems for SMEs and charge them per each implementation. Starting at $25K a pop.

  • We then launch a B2C cloud solution, introduce a subscription model with estimated revenue $50 - $100 per user / month.

  • With enough traction we create and open a marketplace for new plugins, agents, and extensions, like Jira or Trello. We also cultivate a developer community to use it and we charge fee on every sale a developer makes, like Apple.
